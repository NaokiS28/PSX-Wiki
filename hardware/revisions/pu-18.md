# PU-18
Used in:
- [SCPH-55xx](/hardware/models/SCPH-55xx)


General notes:
- Minor FMV skipping issues.
- CD-ROM drive re-located on right side of CD bay to prevent overheating issues with the CD drive. Digital servo for focus/tracking introduced, manual gain/bias calibration removed.
- Lens carriage reinforced and power simplified, making FMV skipping issues less prominent. Only revision with vibration damper under the drive.
- A/V direct out and RFU power connector removed.
- Motherboard PCB reduced in size.
- Model numbers synchronized worldwide.
- Two controllers are included with the SCPH-5552, itself based on the SCPH-5502.
